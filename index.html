The setup wizard accepts a Finnhub key for the current page session. The key is excluded from source, storage, diagnostics, URLs, exports, and backups.

Backup and restore

Create one credential-free JSON backup for transfer through AirDrop or Files. Restoring replaces this browser’s complete portable state after validation; it never merges historical datasets.

Help

Portfolio workflow

Corporate actions require your review

The app does not detect or apply splits, reverse splits, ticker changes, spin-offs, mergers, or other corporate actions. Compare your brokerage records with each stored lot and make corrections manually.

How to record an adjustment

Open Holdings, find the affected acquisition lot, and choose Manual adjustment. Update shares, purchase price, or acquisition date only as supported by your records; add an audit note; review the before-and-after values; then save explicitly.

Warnings are non-authoritative

A future unusual-price warning may suggest review, but it must never confirm a corporate action or alter stored portfolio data.
